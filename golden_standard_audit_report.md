| `PI-020` | Confidence Tags (Graphify pattern) – every state claim in protocol documents must carry an explicit confidence level: VERIFIED (backed by terminal log or test), INFERRED (deduced from indirect evidence), ASSUMED (no evidence, reasonable supposition). Origin: safishamsi/graphify confidence tag system for knowledge graphs. |

Origin: Karpathy LLM Wiki gist, Lint operation of the agent-maintained wiki framework. |
