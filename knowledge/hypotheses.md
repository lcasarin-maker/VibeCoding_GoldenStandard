# Hypotheses

Use this page for observations that still need evidence.

## Minimum record

- `claim`
- `why_it_matters`
- `next_test`
- `evidence`
- `confirmation_count`

## Lifecycle

- `1-2 confirmations`: keep as hypothesis
- `3+ confirmations`: promote to a catalog entry or wiki rule
- `contradicted`: demote or archive with the contradiction source

The live working lane for new hypotheses is `Inbox/`.
