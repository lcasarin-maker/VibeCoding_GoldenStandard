# PR-090: Satellite Insight

**Type:** Promotion candidate

### Insight Description
> Minimal and real exclusions – whitelists, excludes, skips, xfails, stubs, mocks, and placeholders only with verifiable cause; false coverage is debt, not progress.

### Mapping to GS Operational Lenses
*   **[[Domains/D11|Operational Lens 11]]** (Project: *reference*): Keep exclusions minimal and auditable so the security posture stays real instead of ceremonial.
*   **[[Domains/D6|Operational Lens 6]]** (Project: *reference*): Allow exclusions only when they are minimal, justified and real; do not use theater constructs to simulate progress.
*   **[[Domains/D8|Operational Lens 8]]** (Project: *reference*): Reject fake coverage patterns such as xfail-as-expected, placeholder tests, mocks without intent or broad skips without evidence.
*   **[[Domains/D9|Operational Lens 9]]** (Project: *reference*): Prefer discriminative tests over symbolic coverage; if a test cannot fail for the right reason, it is not protecting the system.
