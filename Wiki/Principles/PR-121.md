## Doctrine Snapshot

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Symptom** | Agent picks a direction arbitrarily when uncertain, producing a plausible but wrong implementation that needs to be reverted later. |
| **Cause** | LLM trained to produce a response even under uncertainty; no explicit threshold for stopping. |
| **Solution** | B16: detect confidence below 70% or approach collision. Write both options to AUDIT_TRAIL.md with reasoning. Ask for human decision before proceeding. |
| **Action** | When confidence in the correct approach is below 70%, or when two valid approaches collide without a clear tiebreaker, STOP and escalate to the human operator. Document the conflict, the two options, and the missing information needed to resolve. Do not guess. |
