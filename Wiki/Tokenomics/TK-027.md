### Symptom (Signal)
> Context loss from verbose, repetitive error traces

### Cause
Blind truncation of history from exceeding physical limits

### Application / Mitigation
Apply lexical-redundancy filters (controlled elision) to execution-environment traces

### Operational Relevance
Measurement-telemetry topology validation keeps the diagnostic-compression lens published and linked.

### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# Repetitive error traces (same frame x500) saturate the context.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Controlled elision of lexical redundancy in the traces.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Verbose/repetitive error traces without lexical compression (elision).

### 📚 External evidence
- **vexp.dev / HumanLayer ACE** — unstructured context consumes 3-5x more tokens; selective retrieval, caching, and compaction are the central levers.
