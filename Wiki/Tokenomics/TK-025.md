### Symptom (Signal)
> Fix/revert loops consume the session

### Cause
Unmeasured rework

### Application / Mitigation
Record the rollback cost

### Operational Relevance
Record the cost of fix/revert loops so it does not silently consume the session; the canonical measurement-and-telemetry guidance must stay published and linked from the index, which a CI link-check verifies.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)

```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)

# Record the rollback cost to make it visible.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Invisible rollback (fix/revert) cost, without telemetry to record it.
