### Symptom (Signal)

### Cause
Prioritization not separated

### Application / Mitigation
Send side findings to an external queue

### Operational Relevance
The backlog must be externalized to a durable machine-readable store rather than living only in chat; a test verifies the external backlog artifact exists.
