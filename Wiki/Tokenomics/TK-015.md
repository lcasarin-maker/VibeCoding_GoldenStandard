### Symptom (Signal)
> Redundant few-shot

### Cause
Unnecessary repetition

### Application / Mitigation
Use minimal, diverse, and dense examples

### Operational Relevance
Use minimal, diverse, dense examples so few-shot context is not redundant; the canonical input-and-retrieval guidance must stay published and linked from the index, which a CI link-check verifies.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# 12 nearly identical few-shot examples saturate the prompt.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# A few minimal, diverse, and dense examples.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Redundant few-shot (similar examples) instead of a few diverse, dense ones.

### 📚 External evidence
- **[[Evidence/vexp_dev_humanlayer_ace|vexp.dev / HumanLayer ACE]]** — unstructured context consumes 3-5x more tokens; selective retrieval, caching, and compaction are the central levers.
