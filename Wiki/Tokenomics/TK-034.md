### Symptom (Signal)
> The per-session cost grows silently because it is never compacted or measured; long sessions pay 3-5x more tokens than structured work.


### Cause
Without periodic compaction or cost telemetry, the context grows monotonically and each turn drags along the entire history.


### Application / Mitigation
Compact at usage thresholds, measure the per-session cost, and operate with a budget and alerts.


### Operational Relevance
Per-session token accumulation must be bounded; when output exceeds the efficiency budget, execution is blocked and compaction forced.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# 4-hour session without compacting; the context reaches 95% and the cost skyrockets.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Compaction at thresholds + budget with alerts + per-session cost telemetry.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Long sessions without compaction events or cost telemetry; monotonic context growth. Related to TK-022 (compaction) and TK-031 (unmeasured savings).

### 📚 External evidence
- **[[Evidence/vexp_dev|vexp.dev]]** — unstructured vibe coding costs ~3-5x more (~$300-450/month vs ~$90-135/month structured).
