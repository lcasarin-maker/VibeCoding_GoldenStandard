### Symptom (Signal)
> Redundant few-shot

### Cause
Unnecessary repetition

### Application / Mitigation
Use minimal, diverse, and dense examples

### Operational Relevance
Output-compaction topology validation keeps the compaction lens visible so example bloat is tracked instead of hidden.

### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# 12 nearly identical few-shot examples saturate the prompt.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# A few minimal, diverse, and dense examples.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Redundant few-shot (similar examples) instead of a few diverse, dense ones.

### 📚 External evidence
- **vexp.dev / HumanLayer ACE** — unstructured context consumes 3-5x more tokens; selective retrieval, caching, and compaction are the central levers.
