### Symptom (Signal)
> A long session degrades

### Cause
Saturated finite window

### Application / Mitigation
Summarize state and restart with continuity

### Operational Relevance
Summarize state and restart with continuity so a long session does not degrade; the canonical memory-and-headroom guidance must stay published and linked from the index, which a CI link-check verifies.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text

```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Summarize the state and restart with continuity (compaction).
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Long sessions without periodic compaction that degrade quality.

### 📚 External evidence
