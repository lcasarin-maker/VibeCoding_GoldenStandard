### Symptom (Signal)
> Contradictory copies are re-read

### Cause
Canonical state not defined

### Application / Mitigation
Consult one canonical core and archive copies as evidence

### Operational Relevance
Consult one canonical core and archive copies as evidence so contradictory duplicates are not re-read; the canonical guidance must stay published and linked from the index, which a CI link-check verifies.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text

```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# One canonical core; copies are archived as evidence, not consulted.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Multiple sources of truth that force re-reading contradictory copies.
