# CD03 — Legibility & Causal Explainability

Makes code, assertions, and protocol prose readable enough to expose causal structure instead of hiding it behind opaque naming or unexplained failure output.

---

## Graph Role

Semantic bridge between doctrine and graph readability: improves whether the graph edges and their surrounding prose are cognitively inspectable.

---

## Covers

- Descriptive names and low-noise topology
- Actionable failure messages
- Confidence-tagged, causally explicit protocol prose

---

## Explicitly Excludes

- Dead code detection
- Token budgeting
- General discourse style independent of evidence

### Linked Principles

*   **[[Principles|PR-080]]** (Project: *pytest-good-assertions*): Require failure messages that explain the discrepancy with enough clarity to debug without guesswork.
*   **[[Principles|PR-089]]** (Project: *reference*): Prefer descriptive names and simple topology so purpose is visible at first glance.
