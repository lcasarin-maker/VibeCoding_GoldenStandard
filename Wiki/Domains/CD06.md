# CD06 — Failure Handling & Recovery Paths

Turns failure into a structured operational path with visible next steps, actionable diagnostics, and zero silent debt advancement.

---

## Graph Role

Failure-oriented bridge node: links doctrine to what happens when the graph surfaces a real break instead of a decorative warning.

---

## Covers

- Angry-path discipline
- Structured next steps and recovery paths
- Warnings treated as operational errors until resolved

---

## Explicitly Excludes

- Security scanning as a separate concern
- Token efficiency
- Coverage discrimination

---

### Linked Principles

*   **[[Principles/PR-084|PR-084]]** (Project: *reference*): Turn failure handling into a structured protocol with next steps, evidence, and a visible recovery path.
*   **[[Principles/PR-087|PR-087]]** (Project: *reference*): Treat warnings and known non-blocking findings as operational errors until they are fixed or explicitly blocked.
*   **[[Principles/PR-113|PR-113]]** (Project: *reference*): When behavior is stochastic, define sampling, thresholds, and repetition criteria instead of declaring correctness from one lucky run.
