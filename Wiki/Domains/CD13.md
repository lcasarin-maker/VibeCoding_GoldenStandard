# CD13 — Knowledge Canonicalization & Ingestion

Preserves GS as a live, deduplicated doctrine by normalizing new lessons and forcing explicit uncertainty handling before they become canon.

---

## Graph Role

Knowledge backbone of the graph: protects hub coherence by ensuring new nodes enter through normalized, deduplicated doctrine.

---

## Covers

- Canonical intake of new lessons
- Deduplication and normalization before doctrine promotion
- Explicit uncertainty and semantic consistency across docs

---

## Explicitly Excludes

- Satellite version parity
- Live runtime telemetry
- Generic code complexity
