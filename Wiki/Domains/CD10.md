# CD10 — Test Falsifiability & Assertion Quality

Makes tests fail for the right reason, with precise assertions and honest labeling when no physical gate can yet exist.

---

## Graph Role

Falsifiability anchor: prevents the graph from confusing the existence of a test file with the existence of real protection.

---

## Covers

- Diagnostic assertion quality
- Honest `DOC_ONLY` labeling for non-falsifiable lessons
- Discriminative tests that fail for the claimed reason

---

## Explicitly Excludes

- Raw amount of coverage
- Dependency declarations
- Runtime security scanning

---

### Linked Principles

*   **[[Principles/PR-080|PR-080]]** (Project: *pytest-good-assertions*): Keep assertion output precise and inspectable so a failing test explains the discrepancy instead of performing theater.
*   **[[Principles/PR-094|PR-094]]** (Project: *reference*): Mark non-falsifiable lessons as DOC_ONLY instead of pretending automatic coverage exists.
*   **[[Principles/PR-095|PR-095]]** (Project: *reference*): Avoid many-to-one guards that claim to protect many vices but cannot isolate the real failure.
