# CD07 — Boundary Hygiene & Anti-Theater

Protects the system against ceremonial progress by enforcing minimal real exclusions, justified handoffs, and anti-theater guard design.

---

## Graph Role

Semantic hygiene hub: keeps graph links honest by forcing the repository to justify exceptions instead of normalizing theater.

---

## Covers

- Justified exclusions and minimal bypasses
- Boundary discipline between doctrine, execution, and evidence
- Anti-theater control over mocks, placeholders, and ceremonial guards

---

## Explicitly Excludes

- Dependency CVEs
- Generic security scanning
- Raw coverage quantity

---

### Linked Principles

*   **[[Principles/PR-090|PR-090]]** (Project: *reference*): Allow exclusions only when they are minimal, justified, and real; false coverage is debt, not progress.
*   **[[Principles/PR-093|PR-093]]** (Project: *reference*): Require each new guard to break a real circularity and reduce the baseline instead of merely naming the problem.
*   **[[Principles/PR-095|PR-095]]** (Project: *reference*): Split broad coverage theater into discriminative checks so one guard never pretends to cover many unrelated failures.
