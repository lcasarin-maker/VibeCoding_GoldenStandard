# CD09 — Coverage Adequacy

Asks whether active logic is actually covered by meaningful guards and whether new protection reduces real uncovered surface.

---

## Graph Role

Coverage-to-graph connector: domain edges here help determine which active graph surfaces remain unprotected.

---

## Covers

- Whether relevant behavior is covered at all
- Coverage planning for high-risk or circularity-heavy areas
- Adequate evaluation of stochastic behavior

---

## Explicitly Excludes

- Assertion quality inside an existing test
- Generic code complexity
- Dependency posture
