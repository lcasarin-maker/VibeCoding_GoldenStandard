# CD02 — Completeness & State Continuity

Keeps the operational contract complete by making state, scope, evidence, and checkpoints explicit before and during execution.

---

## Graph Role

Bridge domain between doctrine and execution flow: it keeps principles connected to durable state instead of chat memory.

---

## Covers

- Missing required artifacts or incomplete operational contracts
- State continuity across long runs
- Preflight scope declaration and controlled execution boundaries

---

## Explicitly Excludes

- Naming clarity as a stylistic concern
- Runtime telemetry and post-run evidence streams
- Dependency freshness

---

### Linked Principles

*   **[[Principles/PR-084|PR-084]]** (Project: *reference*): Store state, evidence, checkpoints, and control signals outside the chat so the operational contract stays complete.
*   **[[Principles/PR-086|PR-086]]** (Project: *reference*): Batch predictable authorizations and clarifications before long runs so the control plane can execute without interruptions.
*   **[[Principles/PR-104|PR-104]]** (Project: *reference*): Declare scope, foreseeable impacts, and affected runners before execution, then record new work in backlog instead of reopening scope reactively.
