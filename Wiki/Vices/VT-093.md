### Symptom
> Comments pass

### Cause
Metadata in place of behavior

### Solution
Cross-check usage, call, and test

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Cross-check usage, calls, and tests; docstrings are not evidence of quality.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```python
assert func.__doc__   # has a docstring = 'quality'
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```python
# cross-check usage, call, and a real test of the symbol
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Counting docstrings as a quality signal without verifying behavior.
