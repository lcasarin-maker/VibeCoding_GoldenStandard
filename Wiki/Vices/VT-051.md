### Symptom
> Failures do not block

### Cause
Signal without control

### Solution
Critical failures block

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Critical CI failures must block; an informational-only pipeline lets failures through.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```yaml
# CI shows failures but the merge is not blocked
- run: pytest || true
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```yaml
- run: pytest   # a critical failure blocks the merge (required check)
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
CI that reports but does not block the merge on critical failures.
