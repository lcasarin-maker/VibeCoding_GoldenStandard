### Symptom
> Warnings do not affect approval

### Cause
Incomplete objective function

### Solution
Warnings count as a failure until explicitly classified

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Audited by DeepForensicAuditor D8 and D9 behavioral and static test validations.

### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```python
score = failures   # warnings do not count in the score
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```python
# warnings count as a failure until explicitly classified
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
A quality score that excludes warnings without classifying them.
