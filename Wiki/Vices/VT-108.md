### Symptom
> A component named with a number or descriptor that does not reflect its current implementation

### Cause
Missing semantic contract between the declared name and real behavior

### Solution
A sentinel that compares the formal declaration (name, domain count, version) against the real implementation; fails if they diverge

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Audited by DeepForensicAuditor D8 and D9 behavioral and static test validations.

### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# 'auditor_12d' declares 12 domains but implements 8.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# sentinel compares name, domain count, and version
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Name/formal declaration misaligned with the real implementation.
