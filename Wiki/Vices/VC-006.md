### Symptom
> Work lives in the chat

### Cause
Ephemeral state

### Solution
Keep a stable physical or logical plan

### Corrective Action / Prevention
The plan must be externalized: topology validation requires the inbox surface to be published, linked from the home page, and backed by an ingestion protocol and templates.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# The 6-step plan lives only in the chat; on compaction it is lost.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Versioned Sprint Document (e.g., PLAN.md): steps, status, and done-criteria outside the chat.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Multi-step work without a persistent plan artifact (e.g., Sprint Document / PLAN.md / issue) 
outside the chat.
