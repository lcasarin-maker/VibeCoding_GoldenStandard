### Symptom
> Large edits degrade coherence

### Cause
Scattered mutation

### Solution
Control scope and checkpoints

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Regenerated audit artifacts and topology checks prevent the Home, graph, and index surfaces from drifting away from the YAML catalogs.

### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# Successive massive edits without a checkpoint -> real state diverges from expected.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Verifiable slices + checkpoint (git commit) between edits.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Series of large edits without intermediate commits/checkpoints that allow resuming.
