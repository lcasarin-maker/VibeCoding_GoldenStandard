### Symptom
> Actors execute actions outside their role

### Cause
Unmodeled authorization policy

### Solution
Declare capabilities, limits, and denials per role

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Behavioral/doctrinal vice — not statically falsifiable in a generic way. Documented in the Golden Standard catalogs as governance knowledge; no automated test can discriminate this without human semantic judgment. Sprint 3.4 triage: reclassified from AUDITED/test_behavioral_compliance to DOC_ONLY.

### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```python
def delete_user(actor, uid):
    db.delete(uid)         # without verifying the actor's role/capability
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```python
def delete_user(actor, uid):
    require(actor.can("user.delete"))   # declared and verified capability
    db.delete(uid)
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Sensitive actions without capability/role verification, or the absence of a declared
authorization matrix (capabilities, limits, and denials per role).
