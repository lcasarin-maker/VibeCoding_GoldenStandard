### Symptom
> Multiple files claim to govern agent behavior (BOOTSTRAP, RULES, PERMISSIONS, DEFINITION_OF_DONE, per-agent .md files)

### Cause
Files added incrementally without consolidation; each session adds a new one

### Solution
Collapse all agent governance into .agents/AGENTS.md; all other files are deleted (not bridged, not imported)

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Audit .agents/ and project root for governance files outside AGENTS.md; delete or absorb them.

### 🔎 Concrete detection
audit.py: assert no BOOTSTRAP.md, RULES.md, PERMISSIONS.md, DEFINITION_OF_DONE.md, or [agent].md files exist outside .agents/AGENTS.md
