### Symptom
> The hour or day decides

### Cause
Non-determinism

### Solution
Explicit control of time

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Control time explicitly; a test whose result depends on the wall-clock hour or day is non-deterministic.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```python
start(); time.sleep(1); assert done()   # flaky due to wall-clock
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```python
# explicit control of time or wait by condition
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Tests that depend on sleeps/wall-clock instead of explicit control of time.
