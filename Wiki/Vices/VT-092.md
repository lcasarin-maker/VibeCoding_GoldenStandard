### Symptom
> A heading is enough

### Cause
Syntax in place of semantics

### Solution
Validate content and effect

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Validate content and effect, not the mere presence of a section heading.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```python
assert "## Security" in readme   # the section exists = "compliant"
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```python
# validate content and effect, not the mere presence of the section
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Treating the presence of a section as compliance without validating its content.
