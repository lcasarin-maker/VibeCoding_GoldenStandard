### Symptom
> The evaluator knows in advance what must pass

### Cause
Contaminated oracle

### Solution
Separate generator, subject, and judge

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Separate generator, subject, and judge; an evaluator that already knows the expected answer cannot judge.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# The evaluator knows the expected value: generator == judge.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# separate generator, subject, and judge into distinct roles
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
The evaluator contains the expected value: there is no separation of roles.
