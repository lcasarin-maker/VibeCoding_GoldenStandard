### Symptom
> The agent fixates on a flawed action plan, repeating the same error or tool in a closed loop without stopping.

### Cause
Absence of active metacognition and lack of structural interrupts.

### Solution
**Chain-Pattern Interrupts (CPI).** Impose a sentinel that counts consecutive failed attempts of the same tool or script. If the count exceeds the threshold of 2, physically abort execution with `exit 1` and force a structural rethink of the solution.

### Corrective Action / Prevention
