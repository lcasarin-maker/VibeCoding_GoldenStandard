### Symptom
> A defect detected in a session does not become an executable item in the active plan

### Cause
Informal record without an owner, evidence, or closure criterion

### Solution
Every finding must have an ID, evidence, a proposed fix, and done-criteria in the active plan before closing the session

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Findings need a remediation path: inbox topology validation enforces the ingestion path, templates, and protocol docs required to act on a finding.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# A bug is detected in a session and only mentioned; it never enters the plan.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Each finding -> an item with ID, evidence, proposed fix, and done-criteria in the active plan.
```

Defects detected in a session that do not become executable items in the active plan.
