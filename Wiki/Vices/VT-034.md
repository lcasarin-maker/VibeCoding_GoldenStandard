### Symptom
> The evaluator accepts an incomplete structure

### Cause
Form confused with compliance

### Solution
Require observable behavior before approving

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Require observable behavior before approval; an evaluator that accepts an incomplete structure rubber-stamps a placeholder.
