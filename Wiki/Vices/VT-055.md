### Symptom
> Invisible failure

### Cause
Dead channel

### Solution
Alerts with an owner

### Corrective Action / Prevention
A failure alert needs an owner; an unattended notification makes the failure invisible.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# The broken-test alert goes to a channel nobody reads.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Alerts with an explicit owner and an attention SLA.
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Failure alerts without an assigned owner to attend to them.
