### Symptom
> Modifying governance or protocol files in a satellite repository in isolation without integrating them back into the central core, breaking the ecosystem's operational parity.

### Cause
Governance based on passive physical copying without active drift validation.

### Solution
Implement an active D12 drift-detection gate in the central auditor that compares checksums and blocks if there are deviations in satellites.

### Corrective Action / Prevention
A drift gate must compare checksums across satellite repositories and block when governance files diverge from the central core.
