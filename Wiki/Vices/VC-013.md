### Symptom
> The next actor does not know state, risk, or next step

### Cause
Incomplete state transfer

### Solution
Structured handoff with goal, evidence, and blockers

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Static detector `vc013_ambiguous_handoff` flags handoff notes that omit goal/state/evidence/blockers/next.

### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# Handoff without the minimal fields.
"continue where we left off"
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# Structured, verifiable handoff.
goal: X | state: Y | evidence: .protocol/... | blockers: Z | next: W
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
A handoff/STATUS document that does not contain the required sections (goal,
state, evidence, blockers, next action).
