### Symptom
> Green suites that hide broken or inactive tests

### Cause
Test names outside the oracle's dynamic conventions

### Solution
Mandatory audit of physical correspondence between test files and discovered ones

### Corrective Action / Prevention
Audit the physical correspondence between test files on disk and tests actually discovered, so a green suite cannot hide inactive ones.


### ❌ Example of the vice (Bad)
```text
# tests/ does not physically cover all modules of src/.
```

### ✅ Corrected version (Good)
```text
# file-to-file physical correspondence audit
```

### 🔎 Concrete detection
Test discovery that does not physically cover all modules of the subject.
